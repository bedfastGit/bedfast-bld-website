H7 Flow - Cl.2

Second iteration of the custom loop for my personal rig.
New clear tubbing, CryoFuel Mystic Fog coolant, temp sensor & some Rotary offsets from EKWB. Also swapped out the FLT mounting position to the top rad for easier maintenance.
Fan curve for the top fans is now a mix of two, the first based on the coolant temperature and the other one on GPU temps, it's important to keep in mind that the fans on the rad act as the only exhaust.

Check out Fan Control, it's been a game changer for me.


CPU R5 5600X
Mobo Asus Rog Strix B550f Gaming
Memory 2x8GB @3000MT/s cl16 G.skill Trident Z RGB
G. Card MSI RTX 3080 10GB Gaming X Trio

Samsung 860 evo 500GB SATA SSD x2
Cooling EKWB Velocity2 Custom loop
PSU Seasonic Focus GX-750 Gold
Case NZXT H7 Flow black
