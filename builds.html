4000X - Anime build

Gaming & workstation for 3D art.

The goal was to give this rig a new look, in line with both the work it's going to perform and the personality of the artist.

You can follow her work at @3d.kat or visit her portfolio on this link.


*Note: The G. card will hopefully be upgraded at some point in the near future.


Specs: Ryzen 5 3600 || 16GB RAM 2x8GB @3000MHz cl16 Corsair Vengeance || Mobo Asus Strix B550-A || G.card Sapphire RX 580 8GB || Case Corsair 4000X white - anime sticker mod || PSU Corsair CV650 80 plus bronze || Boot drive Kingston nvme SSD 240GB + 1TB 7200rpm Seagate Barracuda || Cooling Noctua NH-U12S Chromax Black w/ sticker mod


S340 Elite - Personal Rig

Personal Rig undate after cleaning and reasembling. I reorganized the cablemanagement from scratch too.

I'm planning on multiple upgrades such as changing to a newer platform, most likely Z690, as well as a case swap looking forward to make a custom loop for my personal system.


*Note: my primary display is 1440p 144Hz, so my CPU is not a huge problem in most games. It will be the first thing to upgrade though.


Specs: Ryzen 7 2700 || 16GB RAM 2x8GB @3000MHz cl16 G.skill Trident Z RGB || Mobo MSI B450 gaming pro carbon ac || G.card MSI RTX 3080 10GB Gaming X Trio || Case NZXT S340 elite black || PSU Seasonic Focus GX-750 Gold || Storage Samsung 860 evo 500GB SATA SSD || Boot drive Samsung 860 evo 500GB SATA SSD || Cooling Noctua NH-D15 Chromax Black || Anti-sag bracket || Antec sleeved extensions || x2 Nf-a14 Chromax as front intake || x1 nf-a12x25 chromax as rear exhaust || x1 nf-a15 Chromax as top exhaust


Meshlicious - edit. Rig

Video editing SFF build in the SSUPD Meshlicious featuring a 280mm rad in the front as an exhaust and G.card mounted vertically pulling in fresh air through the side panel.

The client requested a small PC so it could be moved more easily between their two places of work, while still being capable of light video editing whit good thermals and noise levels.


*Note: we repurposed some parts from the old client's rig such as the ram kit (to be upgraded when needed).


Specs: Ryzen 7 3700X || 16GB RAM 2x8GB @3200MHz cl16 Corsair Vengeance || Mobo Aorus B550i Pro AX || G.card Asus Strix GTX 1070 8GB || Case SSUPD Meshlicious || PSU Corsair SF600 80 plus platinum || Boot drive Samsung 1TB 980pro nvme PCIe 4.0 || Cooling EK AIO 280 D-RGB


H510 - gaming Rig

Midrange gaming build, core components purchased in 2017, still makes of a good gaming system, especially considering the current situation regarding G.cards pricing among other pieces of hardware.

This was a change of Case for an old system with some aditions to upgrade its aesthetics.


*Note: I'm aware that some components are somewhat outdated, not everyone needs the newest hardware though, keep that in mind please.


Specs: Intel i7 7700k || 16GB RAM 2x8GB @2400MHz cl16 Corsair Vengeance || Mobo Asus Rog Strix Z270-f gaming || G.card Asus Strix GTX 1070 8GB OC || Case NZXT H510 white || PSU EVGA GQ 650W 80 plus Gold || Storage Seagate Barracuda 1TB 7200rpm || Boot drive Kingston M.2 Sata SSD 120GB || Cooling Noctua NH-U12S Chromax Black || Anti-sag bracket
