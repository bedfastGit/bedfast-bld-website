This is bedfast_bld!


I'm a tech enthusiast focusing on PC hardware, I throw myself into every build to achieve the high standards expected from a proffesional custom build. I tend to every new rig as my own keeping the main focus on acustics, cooling and performance while still being aesthetically appealing.

In this website you'll find both visual and technical information about some of my main work, I hope you enjoy it and if there's anything you'd like to know, do not hesitate to reach me through your prefered service.



Superior performance

Every build specs are designed specifically for each client's needs, prioritizing performance and cooling while keeping the noise levels down for a better working experience.


Clean & tidy

All builds are proffesionally cable managed keeping everything as hidden and tidy as possible. This way we achieve a clean aesthetic while keeping cables out of the way for airflow and fans in certain scenarios such us SFF builds.


It's not just the tower

From mouse to headset we've got you covered, it is as important as your hardware to have an appropiate set of peripherals. A monitor can make a bigger difference than a graphics card upgrade.

The important thing is the experience not the specs!
